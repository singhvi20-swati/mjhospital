About Us

A Hospital Built for Children

Where expert medical care meets a warm, welcoming environment designed to put children and parents at ease.

Trusted Pediatric Specialists in Surat

MJ Children Hospital is dedicated to providing comprehensive healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents. Our child-friendly facility features a calming, ocean-themed environment that helps little ones feel comfortable during their visit.

Led by two highly qualified pediatricians with specialized fellowships in neonatal care and pediatric ICU, we combine clinical excellence with genuine compassion for every family we serve.
